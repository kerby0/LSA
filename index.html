Services

Food Production

Farmers are the primary producers of food, including fruits, vegetables, grains, meat, and dairy products, ensuring a stable food supply for communities.

Raw Material Supply

Farmers supply raw materials for various industries, such as cotton for textiles, timber for construction, and crops for biofuel production.

Water Management

Farmers play a crucial role in managing water resources efficiently, including implementing irrigation systems, rainwater harvesting, and responsible water use practices.

Economic Contribution

AAgriculture is a significant contributor to national economies, generating income through the sale of crops, livestock, and other agricultural products.
